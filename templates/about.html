A Student Grade Management System, often referred to as a Gradebook or Grading System, is a software application or platform designed to help educational institutions, teachers, and administrators efficiently manage, record, and analyze student grades and academic performance. It streamlines the grading process, reduces administrative work, and enhances transparency and communication between students, parents, and educators. It is a valuable tool for managing academic performance and supporting student success in educational institutions of all levels, from K-12 schools to universities. {% endblock %}
